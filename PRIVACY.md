# Privacy Policy for RenameTab

RenameTab is designed to rename Chrome tab titles locally in your browser.

## Data Collection

RenameTab does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal information.

The extension does not use analytics, does not contact external servers, and does not require an account.

## Local Browser Storage

RenameTab stores the following data locally using Chrome extension storage:

- The custom tab title you enter.
- The tab URL associated with a custom title when needed for the selected persistence mode.
- Your default title persistence setting.

This data stays in Chrome extension storage on your device. It is not sent to the developer or to any third party.

## Permissions

RenameTab requests permissions only to:

- Apply a custom title to the active tab.
- Keep or clear the custom title according to the selected persistence mode.
- Store your local settings.

## Contact

For privacy questions or support, please open an issue in the project repository.
